A new captcha method that is more accessible.

How it works: There are two different modes: verification and captcha.

In verification mode, the user swipes their keyboard in a line, one key at a time, and if their swipe matches the pattern of their past swipes, they are able to log in.

In captcha mode, the user swipes their keyboard in a line just like verification mode, however the swipe is compared to a perfect AI swipe to determine if it is human or not.
